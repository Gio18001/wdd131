Pseudoregalia Game

Pseudoregalia is a 3D platforming metroidvania game with an incredible movement system. Being a metroidvania, you begin the game with a very limited set of abilities, and are barred access from most places. As you progress in the game, you unlock new abilities that allow you to gain access to previously blocked off areas. As you gain the ability to slide, long jump, kick off walls, and more, it becomes one of the most satifying movement systems in gaming today.

You control a goat/butler/maid/rabbit character named Sybil, and traverse a large and often confusing map while defeating enemies and solving puzzles to gain new abilities or access to new areas. There are many rooms to explore, and finding them all is a challenge in itself.
