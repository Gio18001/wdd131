Where did Pseudoregalia come from?

Pseudoregalia began as the winner of the Metroidvania Month 19 Jam on Itch.io. Even though it started as a simple tech demo for a game jam, it gained popularity so quickly that the developer, Rittzler, decided to refine and finish it to release as a full game.
